Your two options at maturity

Convert into equity — or take your money back.

📈
Option A — Become a shareholder
Convert your investment into shares of Sencilo at a 35% discount to the price paid by seed investors. Early believers get a better price than the institutional money that comes in later.
Example — CHF 500 invested:
Seed round at CHF 2.0M pre-money. Seed price = CHF 2.0 per share (illustrative). Your price = CHF 1.30 per share (35% less). Your CHF 500 buys ~385 shares instead of ~250. You own a small but real piece of Sencilo.
🔄
Option B — Get your money back, with interest
If you'd rather not convert, we repay your full principal plus 5% per year simple interest for the time your money was with us. No questions asked.
Example — CHF 500 for 18 months:
Interest = CHF 500 × 5% × 1.5 years = CHF 37.50. You receive CHF 537.50 back. Your capital was never at risk of not returning — the only question is whether you want upside.

⚠ This is an early-stage investment. Option B (repayment) depends on Sencilo's financial position at maturity. In the unlikely event the company has failed, your capital may not be recoverable. Invest only what you can afford to lose, and only if you believe in what we're building.

Nature of this agreement

This page is shared privately with individuals known to the founders. It does not constitute a public offer or solicitation of securities under Swiss law (FinSA / FinIA). The instrument described is a private convertible loan (Darlehen mit Wandlungsrecht) governed by Swiss law (Code of Obligations / OR).

Instrument summary

Investors lend a principal amount (minimum CHF 100) to Sencilo. The loan bears interest at 5% per annum (simple, not compounded) from the date of transfer. The loan matures at the close of Sencilo's first formal seed financing round (the "Qualified Financing"). At maturity, the investor may elect either: (a) conversion into equity at a 30% discount to the seed round price per share, or (b) repayment of principal plus accrued interest. The investor's choice must be communicated within 30 days of written notice from Sencilo of the Qualified Financing close.

Pool cap

The aggregate principal raised under this instrument is capped at CHF 75,000. This is calibrated so that, assuming conversion at a CHF 2.0M pre-money seed valuation with a 30% discount, all early investors collectively hold no more than approximately 5% of the company at conversion. Sencilo will not accept investments that would cause the aggregate to exceed this cap.

Risk warning

This is an early-stage investment. The repayment option (Option B) depends on Sencilo's financial position at maturity. If the company has not raised a seed round and has insufficient cash, repayment may be delayed or impossible. Invest only amounts you are prepared to lose in full. The conversion option (Option A) results in equity in a private, illiquid company — there is no guarantee of future value or exit.

No prospectus

No prospectus has been prepared. This page is not a prospectus within the meaning of FinSA. Investors are encouraged to seek independent financial and legal advice before investing.

Governing law

The convertible loan agreement is governed by Swiss law. Jurisdiction: Canton of Vaud.
