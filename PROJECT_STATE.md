### QR invoice capture ‚Äî fully working end-to-end (2026-05-12)

2. **LAN IP in QR URL** ‚Äî `captureSessionService.ts` built the mobile URL from `process.env.FRONTEND_URL || getLocalNetworkIp()`. 6. **Source image ‚Äî invoice detail page** ‚Äî `<img src="/api/invoices/:id/source-file">` also lacked auth. Fixed: same fetch ‚Üí blob URL pattern.
